Hello, I am Eldanya.

I was born in South Africa Mpumulanga. When I was 3 we moved to CapeTown where I was inspired by the artists who sat at the beach sketching people. I always had a passion of creating art and designs. I started exploring with different mediums, like paint, ink and pencil. In highschool I had art, design and engineering graphic design (EGD) as my best subjects.

In matric I got destinctions for all 3 and even won the throphy for the best design student. I then decided to move to Pretoria Gauteng to study graphic design, where I learned how to work many design programs, code websites, film and photograpy. I love learnig about the history of art and design. In my spare time I make tiny clay models that I turn into necklaces. My favourate mediums I do art with are water paint and blue pen sketches.
